About us

STP was founded in 2006 at Cairo university; The purpose of STP is to strengthen the leadership, skills, effectiveness and efficiency of involved people; enabling them to further enrich the quality of the community and their personal life.

Overview

STP "Steps Towards Progress" is a student activity which was held for the first time in the academic year 2005/2006; It is completely managed and organized by students under the supervision of The Association of Friends of the National Cancer-free Intiative (AFNCI), the idea was raised to provide students with the skills needed by the supplementary skills needed in the labor market. STP's Vision is "Being a leading organization working on developing youth who will lead the positive change." STP attempts to deliver values and achieve its vision through 3 different projects: -Conference (Engineering & Management) -Juniors -Events

Vision

To be a Leading organization working on developing youth who will lead positive changes.

Mission

To develop youth in various fields personally and technically through academic programs, projects, and events in a values oriented system, to take our part in providing a generation capable of playing its roles effictively towards itself, community and country.
